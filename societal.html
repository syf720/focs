Using Technology Responsibly

Facts

Did you know that almost 3/4 of Americans have been a victim of cyber crime? It is important to never reveal confidential information on the Internet. This includes but is not excluded to your address, social security number, and credit card number. You should also never use easy to guess passwords or the same password for everything. These practices can make you an easy victim of cyber crime.

Feelings

I think that many of us do not use safe Internet practices. Most people tend to use the same password over and over, while others reveal sensitive information. I think that we ALL need to be more cautious. Something minor could have MAJOR consequences!
